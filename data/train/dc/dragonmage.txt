XAO JIN, known as DRAGONMAGE, is a comic book fictional character, a 
Comics superhero and comes from New Shanghai Colony. He first appeared
in Legion of Super-Heroes (vol. 4) #33, during the 'Five year gap' where
he joined the resistance to Dominator controlled Earthgov.


Fictional character biography

In pre-Zero Hour continuity, Xao Jin became a Legionnaire under the
codename Dragonmage. Xao Jin received training from Master Chu Hua as
a young student in the mystic arts.


Powers and abilities

Xao Jin is a sorcerer whose spells are accompanied by holograms of
Chinese dragons.
