YO-YO RODRIGUEZ, also known as SLINGSHOT, is a fictional character in
.


Publication history

Yo-Yo Rodriguez first appeared in The Mighty Avengers #13 and was
created by Brian Michael Bendis and Alex Maleev.


Fictional character biography

Yo-Yo Rodriguez is the Puerto-Rican daughter of the Griffin. She is
recruited by Nick Fury to join his anti-Skrull task force. This team
becomes known as the Secret Warriors.

Both of her arms are severed by the Gorgon, and she is temporarily
unable to remain active with the team. However, both arms are later
replaced with prosthetics, and she returns to active duty.


Powers and abilities

She can run at superhuman speed, then bounces back to the point where
she began.


In other media

An Inhuman character based on Yo-Yo Rodriguez appears in the Agents of
S.H.I.E.L.D._ episode "Bouncing Back", portrayed by Natalia
Cordova-Buckley. Elena Rodriguez, nicknamed "Yo-Yo" due to her power,
is a "street-wise Colombian woman". She has the ability to move at super
speed for the duration of one heartbeat, always returning to the point
from which she started.
